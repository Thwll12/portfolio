About me

I am an Instructional Designer with over fifteen years of experience in developing instructor led and eLearning training materials and programs. My work reflects a body of work that includes an array of diverse training materials such as facilitator/participant guides, user guides, job aids, infographics, video simulations, and graphic panel novels.

I apply a systematic methodology to develop content, experiences, and other solutions to support the customer's training needs.
