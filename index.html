About Me

I'm an Instructional Design leader with over 10 years of experience transforming complex content into meaningful, accessible learning experiences. From onboarding and compliance to systems training, I specialize in creating solutions that align with business goals and engage adult learners. My toolkit includes Articulate 360, Storyline, Rise, Vyond, and other modern learning technologies. I also have extensive experience managing end-to-end training programs and leading instructional design projects from planning to delivery.

I thrive in remote environments and bring a calm, strategic mindset to fast-paced projects. I'm currently seeking a full-time, remote opportunity where my expertise in learning strategy, employment development, and stakeholder engagement can make a lasting impact.
